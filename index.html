Meet the Team

Josh Bennet

A writer and amateur filmmaker passionate about fighting mental illness through creative expression and helping others do the same.

Josh envisioned *Valuable* after being unable to find a productivity app that aligned with his values. As the project's Creative Lead, he aims to merge creativity and technology while learning programming for a hands-on role. Josh enjoys playing guitar and has worked in the film industry, with a series of short films available on YouTube.

Aaron Clark

A programmer passionate about meditation and philosophy, striving to find his own ikigai through helping others achieve peace of mind.

Aaron founded and led his community college's first software engineering club, excelling academically and obtaining a certificate in computer programming. He then led a mobile app startup and worked as a freelance web developer. As the project's Technical Lead, Aaron brings vision and experience to *Valuable*.
